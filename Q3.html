How to make Tom Yum Soup

Summary

Tom Yum soup is everybody's favourite Thai soup.
There are 2 types of Tom Yum soup; The Classic Clear Tom Yum & the Creamy Tom yum soup.Today we are going to make the Clear Tom Yum soup.

Ingredients

  • Chilli Padi
  • Kaffir lime leaves
  • Galangal
  • Garlic
  • lemongrass
  • Shrimp stock
  • Water
  • Prawns
  • Tomato
  • Coriander
  • Onion
  • Lime juice
  • salt & sugar to taste

Direction

  1. Clean / Devein the prawns
  2. Pounce the garlic & lemongrass
  3. In a pot, bring 3 cups of water to boil. Add the prawns and shimp stock.
  4. Turn the heat and add the lemongrass,galangal,kaffir lime leaves,Chiili padi. Bring to boil.
  5. Lastly, add lime juice and garnish with coriander and cilantro.
